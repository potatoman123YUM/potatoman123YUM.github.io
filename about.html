Who We Are

We are a passionate group of potato enthusiasts who are dedicated to advocating for the humane treatment of potatoes. We believe in sustainable farming, proper cooking methods, and most importantly, no unnecessary harm to potatoes.

Our goal is to educate the public on the importance of treating potatoes with care—from how they’re grown to how they’re cooked. Bad potato recipes hurt our cause, and we will stop at nothing to protect the potato world!
