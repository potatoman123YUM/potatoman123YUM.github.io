Ethical Potatoes

Our mission: We want to make potatoes ethically, prevent bad recipes, and reduce potato waste!

We work to stop the over-mashing of potatoes. A big chunk of mashed potatoes is disgusting, and we are against it. We're also fighting against the illegal use of iPhone 5s in the potato industry. We need to stop blueberries from affecting potato growing and make sure potatoes are cultivated properly.
