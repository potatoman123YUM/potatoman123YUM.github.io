Ethical Potatoes

Our mission: We want to protect potatoes and ensure ethical practices in their preparation!

Over-mashing potatoes is harmful! It destroys the texture and integrity of the potato, and it's just wrong. Plus, big chunks of mashed potatoes are disgusting. We are also fighting to put an end to horrible French fry recipes that make them tasteless and ugly, and we're taking action to stop these bad practices. Together, we'll make potatoes great again!

PETP (People for the Ethical Treatment of Potatoes)

Dear Lay's Team,

I hope this email finds you well. My name is Joshua Ryan, and I am the CEO, Founder, and Investigator at People for the Ethical Treatment of Potatoes (PETP). Our organization is committed to advocating for the ethical treatment and responsible sourcing of potatoes, with our work highlighted on SaveThePotatoes.org.

Lay's has become a household name for potato products, and I am impressed by the consistent quality of your chips. Given our mission, I am reaching out to learn more about your farming practices, including how you ensure ethical and sustainable treatment of potatoes during cultivation and harvesting.

Could you also share information on the quality standards you apply throughout your production process, from sourcing to the final packaging of your products? This insight is vital to PETP's efforts in promoting ethical practices across the potato industry.

Thank you for your time and attention. I look forward to your response.

Best regards,
Joshua Ryan
CEO, Founder, and Investigator
People for the Ethical Treatment of Potatoes (PETP)
SaveThePotatoes.org
