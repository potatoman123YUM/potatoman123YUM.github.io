Ethical French Fry Recipes

Perfect Baked French Fries

A gentle, oil-minimal approach to crispy fries.

  • Clean potatoes with care
  • Cut into even strips
  • Soak in warm water
  • Pat dry with soft towel
  • Bake at 425°F (218°C)

Air-Fried Potato Wedges

A kind approach to crispy wedges.

  • Wash potatoes gently
  • Cut into wedges
  • Season thoughtfully
  • Air fry at 380°F (193°C)
