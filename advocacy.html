Fair Farming & Ethical Cooking

Our advocacy focuses on:

  • Promoting sustainable, ethical farming practices for potatoes.
  • Preventing over-mashing and overly processed potato products.
  • Encouraging people to cook potatoes in ways that respect their natural form and taste.
  • Educating consumers about the detrimental effects of poorly prepared French fries, mashed potatoes, and more.
